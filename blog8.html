The Story Behind Our Coffee: From Bean to Cup

At Burger King, we believe that every great coffee starts with a story. From the fertile lands where our beans are grown to the moment they reach your cup, our coffee undergoes a meticulous process designed to preserve the unique flavors and quality of each bean. Join us on a journey through the art and science of coffee-making, where passion meets precision in every cup we serve.

1. Sourcing the Finest Beans

Our journey begins with a commitment to quality. We source our coffee beans from some of the world’s most renowned coffee-growing regions, including the lush highlands of Central America, South America, and Africa. We partner directly with farmers who share our dedication to sustainable and ethical farming practices, ensuring that every bean we use is grown with care and respect for the environment.

Freshly harvested coffee beans from our partnered farms.

2. Expert Roasting

Once harvested, our beans are shipped to our state-of-the-art roasting facilities, where they are carefully roasted to perfection. Our roasting process is where the magic happens — it brings out the rich, complex flavors that define our coffee. Our master roasters use a blend of time-honored techniques and modern technology to create a balanced roast that highlights the unique characteristics of each bean variety.

The art of roasting: unlocking the flavors within each bean.

3. Grinding for Freshness

We believe that freshness is key to a great cup of coffee. That’s why we grind our beans in small batches to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. The grind size is carefully adjusted to match the brewing method, whether it’s a classic drip coffee, an espresso, or a cold brew. This meticulous attention to detail guarantees that every cup of coffee served at Burger King is as fresh and flavorful as possible.

Grinding our beans to order for a fresh, aromatic brew.

4. Brewing Perfection

Our baristas are trained in the art of coffee-making, using precise brewing methods to extract the perfect flavor from our beans. Whether you’re enjoying a simple black coffee or a more elaborate caramel macchiato, each drink is crafted with care to deliver a satisfying experience with every sip. We constantly refine our brewing techniques to meet the highest standards of quality and consistency.

Brewing the perfect cup: from bean to your cup.

5. Serving with Passion

At Burger King, we don’t just serve coffee; we share a passion. Each cup is a culmination of our dedication to excellence and our love for coffee. We strive to provide a warm, welcoming experience every time you visit, whether you’re grabbing a quick cup on your way to work or settling in for a leisurely chat with friends. Our goal is to make each coffee moment special.

Our baristas are passionate about crafting the perfect coffee experience.

Conclusion

The story behind our coffee is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and exceptional taste. From sourcing the finest beans to serving each cup with care, every step of our process is designed to bring you the best coffee experience possible. So, the next time you savor a cup of coffee at Burger King, remember the journey it took from bean to cup — and enjoy every sip!

Darren Kim, Main Chef
